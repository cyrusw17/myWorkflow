# Priority Roadmap

> One phase at a time. Finish the current phase before opening new projects.

**Current phase**: 2 — MVP Website (built; install PHP & deploy)
**North star**: First paying client → $10k/month recurring path

---

## How priorities are ordered

Your instinct was right: **name → website → get out there**. One correction: don't wait for a perfect site to start outreach. Build an MVP site, then **shift most energy to visibility and sales** immediately.

| # | Priority | Why |
|---|----------|-----|
| 1 | Business name + domain | Everything else attaches to this |
| 2 | MVP website | Credibility for outreach; your portfolio is your product |
| 3 | Get name out there | Revenue only happens when people know you exist |
| 4 | First client | Proof, cash, case study |
| 5 | Recurring revenue systems | Hosting/maintenance billing, onboarding SOP |
| 6 | Scale | Hire, more niches, own infrastructure |

## Phase 3 — Visibility & Outreach (Weeks 2–8, ongoing)

**Goal**: Conversations → proposals → first client.

This becomes the **main weekly focus** once Phase 2 MVP is live. Outreach can start **in parallel** during Phase 2 (conversations don't require a finished site).

### Channels (pick 2 to start, not all 6)

- [ ] **In-person**: Visit local shops with a one-pager or tablet showing examples
- [ ] **Cold outreach**: Email/DM owners of businesses with bad or no sites
- [ ] **Warm network**: Friends, family, acquaintances who know shop owners
- [ ] **Google Maps prospecting**: Find businesses with no site or terrible sites → contact
- [ ] **Referral ask**: Tell everyone what you're doing; ask for introductions
- [ ] **Local groups**: Chamber, Facebook groups, trade associations

### Weekly outreach targets (starting Week 2)

| Metric | Target |
|--------|--------|
| Conversations started | 10/week |
| Follow-ups sent | 5/week |
| Proposals sent | 2/month (early) |

**Exit criteria**: First signed client.

## Phase 5 — Recurring Revenue Systems

- [ ] Standard hosting/maintenance agreement
- [ ] Billing automation (Stripe, etc.)
- [ ] Client onboarding SOP
- [ ] Monthly check-in template
- [ ] Pricing validated from 3+ clients

**Exit criteria**: Predictable MRR, repeatable delivery without reinventing each time.

---

## Phase 6 — Scale (later)

- [ ] Second client via same outreach playbook
- [ ] Raise prices with proof
- [ ] First contractor or employee
- [ ] SEO / automation upsells
- [ ] Own hosting infrastructure

---

## What NOT to do right now

- Register 5 LLCs or buy courses
- Build internal tools before you have clients
- Expand to unrelated niches
- Perfect brand guidelines before first sale
- Quit Discount Tire
