## What we are

A **local business growth partner** that builds custom-coded websites and keeps them running — hosting, maintenance, SEO, automation — so service businesses get more leads without thinking about tech.

**We are NOT:** a generic freelancer selling pages, a cheap Fiverr mill, a full marketing agency (yet), or a SaaS company.

---

## Who we serve

**Primary niche:** Local service businesses  
**Best fit:** Automotive, blue-collar, trades — shops, detailers, mechanics, contractors, HVAC, roofing, plumbing, landscaping

**Ideal client profile:**
- Relies on phone calls and walk-ins
- No website or outdated/broken site
- Owner is busy running the business, not learning tech
- Will pay monthly for someone to handle it all

**Service area:** United States (nationwide)

## Positioning

**One-liner:**
> Groundwork Web builds and runs websites for local service businesses — so you get more leads and never worry about your site again.

**2-sentence pitch:**
> We build custom-coded websites for local shops and trades — automotive, contractors, HVAC, and more. Then we handle hosting and maintenance every month, so you get more calls and never touch the tech.

**Sell outcomes** (more calls, more leads, more trust) — **not** pages, features, or tech stack.

## CTA model (current)

**Application form** — not book-a-call / Calendly-first.

Form collects:
- Name, phone, email (required if email is preferred contact method)
- Business name, city/state, industry
- **Preferred contact method:** phone call, text, or email
- **Best time to contact:** morning, afternoon, evening, anytime

Success message: contact within 24 hours using their preferred method.

## Sales conversation flow

1. **Discover** — What's broken? What does a good month look like?
2. **Diagnose** — Show their site (or lack of one) vs what good looks like
3. **Offer** — Founding program terms; sell outcome not tech
4. **Close** — First month upfront recommended; kickoff date
5. **Follow up** — One more touch after 3 days, then move on
