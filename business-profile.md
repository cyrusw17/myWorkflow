## What We Are

A **local business growth partner** that builds custom-coded websites and keeps them running — hosting, maintenance, SEO, and automation — so service businesses get more leads without thinking about tech.

We are **not** a generic freelancer selling pages. We sell **outcomes**: more calls, more bookings, more trust online.

---

## Who We Serve

**Primary niche**: Local service businesses  
**Best fit**: Automotive, blue-collar, and trades (shops, detailers, mechanics, contractors, HVAC, etc.)

**Ideal client**:
- Relies on phone calls and walk-ins
- Has no website, or an outdated/broken one
- Owner is busy running the business, not learning tech
- Will pay monthly for someone to handle it all

---
